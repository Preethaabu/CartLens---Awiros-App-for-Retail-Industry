The tablet strip contains tablets of DICLOFENAC SODIUM, CHLORZOXAZONE, and PARACETAMOL. Each uncoated tablet contains Diclofenac Sodium IP: 50mg, Paracetamol IP: 325mg, and Chlorzoxazone USP: 250mg. The tablets should be taken as directed by a Physician. These tablets are not meant for veterinary use.

For usage: Take these tablets as per the instructions provided by your healthcare provider.

Explanation: These tablets are a combination medication that helps in relieving pain and reducing inflammation. Diclofenac Sodium is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug, paracetamol is a pain reliever and fever reducer, while chlorzoxazone is a muscle relaxant. Taken together, they can help manage conditions involving pain, inflammation, and muscle spasms. It is important to follow the instructions given by your doctor regarding how and when to take these tablets.
